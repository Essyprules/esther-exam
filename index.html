WELCOME TO GREENSPRINGS SCHOOL

Our mission is to lead holistic, inclusive,
innovative world-class education in Africa. For this
reason, we have adopted different approaches and
tools that make learning enjoyable for our
students. These approaches and tools include the
use of activity-based learning, virtual learning environment,
among others. In addition to that, we
are the first Thinking School in Nigeria,
and we encourage our students to be
critical thinkers.

Girls win games

The 2024 Private Schools Games (U-11) which
was held on Wednesday, May 8 concluded
with remarkable achievements for our Lekki Campus
female football team, who triumphed by securing
first place out of 8 teams. Two
outstanding players from our girls’ team, Oreofeoluwa
Robert and Balogun Hadizath received special
recognition for their exceptional contributions.

Our very own solo vocalist

In a dazzling display of musical prowess,
Elisabeth Oke from our Lekki campus has
clinched the first position in the MUSON
Schools Competition’s Vocal Solo — Intermediate category. The
prestigious event, organized by the Music Society
of Nigeria (MUSON) School of Music, was
held at the MUSON Centre in Onikan,
Lagos, on May 23, 2024.
